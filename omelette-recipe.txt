Ingredients:
 - 3 tablespoon butter
 - 2 eggs
 - 1 onion
 - 1 tomato
 - 1/2 tablespoon chilli powder
 - 1/4 tablespoon turmeric powder
 - 1 tablespoon salt

Directions:
  1. Break two eggs in a bowl and add a finely chopped onion to it.
  2. Now add a finely chopped tomato to it.
  3. After it add chilli powder, turmeric powder and salt in the amount mentioned to it.
  4. Whisk the mixture for 10-15 seconds. Now heat a saucepan on a medium flame and add butter to it and let it melt and spread it all over the pan as it doesn't let the omelette to stick to the pan.
  5. Now when your pan has been heating for a while now add the the mixture to pan and spread it all around the pan in your desired shape and let it there for a 3-4 minutes. After that turn it over and do the same for the other side. Now it is ready to be served. Enjoy!
